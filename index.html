Pricing & process

Every project is scoped and quoted individually — scope, usage rights, location and timeline all affect the final number. These ranges reflect what most projects actually cost, so you know whether we're in the same ballpark before reaching out.

Photography & editorial A$1,200 – A$3,000

Portrait, street, editorial and event shoots. Includes edited high-res gallery and standard licensing. Multi-day and campaign work quoted separately.

Brand content A$2,000 – A$5,000

Photo and video combined in a single shoot day. Social-ready edits, paid-ad usage rights and a pre-shoot strategy call included.

Videography A$2,500 – A$8,000+

Brand films, documentary coverage, reels and event video. Full production, colour grade and one round of revisions. Complex multi-day productions quoted on request.

How it works

  1. 01

    Send an enquiry

    Fill in the form below with your project brief and budget range. The more detail, the faster I can come back to you with a real number.

  2. 02

    Scoping call

    For anything beyond a straightforward shoot, we'll do a short call to align on the brief, timeline and deliverables before any quote is sent.

  3. 03

    Proposal & deposit

    You receive a written proposal with a fixed quote. A 50% deposit secures the date — no date is held without it.

  4. 04

    Shoot & deliver

    We shoot, I edit, you get your files. Typical turnaround is 5–7 business days. Rush delivery available at an additional rate.

The person behind the lens

About

"I'm drawn to the moments between the moments — the pause, the glance, the ordinary made extraordinary by light."

I'm Yu (Jay) Fei — photographer, videographer and content creator based in Sydney, with 7 years behind the lens shooting editorial, street and documentary work for brands, publications and artists who care about how their story is told.

Beyond client work, I share my process through gear reviews, tutorials and long-form content on YouTube — 20,500 subscribers and counting — breaking down the craft without the gatekeeping.

When I'm not shooting, I'm building tools to help other creators work better: camera settings guides, presets, LUTs and courses distilled from years in the field.
